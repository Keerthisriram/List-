Keerthi's Recipe for the best Chicken Biriyani

Ingredients

For making biriyani:

  • 1/2 kg biriyani rice
  • 1 kg Chicken
  • Ghee or oil
  • 3/4 teaspoon Jeera
  • 5 cardamoms
  • 1 or 2 bay leafs
  • Star anise
  • 5 Gloves
  • 2 Onion
  • Ginger Garlic paste
  • 4 Green chillies
  • 3 teaspoon plain yogurt
  • Chilli powder
  • 1 Lemon
  • chopped Mint leaves
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon Garam masala or (biriyani masala)

Preparation:

Steps to soak rice:

  1. Add 2 cups biriyani rice to a large pot and rince it atleast thrice.
  2. Drain in freshwater and soak for 30 mins.

Steps for Chicken Marination:

  1. Add Chicken pieces into a large bowl.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste yogurt,garama masala(biriyani masala),1/2 lemon juice,turmeric powder, chili powder and salt
  3. Mixed everthing well and marinate the chicken.
  4. cover and set this aside for 30 mins.

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee or oil on a heavy bottom pot or pressure cooker
  2. Add bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, jeera and star anise
  3. Add thinly slice onion and fry them until uniformly light brown
  4. Add marinated Chicken and saute until it becomes pale for 5 mins
  5. Cover and cook until chicken is soft, tender and completely cooked
  6. Add Green chillis and add required salt, biriyani masala or garam masala, yogurt and chopped mint leaves
  7. Mix everthing well and spread it evenly in a single layer
  8. Layered drained rice all over the chicken and add 3 cups water and add mint leaves.
  9. Finally cover the pot or cooker
  10. If cooking in cooker, cook until hear 1 whistle
  11. If cooking in pot, cook on a medium heat until the rice is cooked completely.
